Uncorrelated by design. Compounded by discipline.

I believe in diversification — by asset and class, by strategy, by style, and by timeframe. A basket of stocks isn’t enough. Running many uncorrelated edges was once a hedge fund’s privilege. Not anymore. This is where I build my own.
